I'm trying to write the first performance review for an employee who doesn't sleep, doesn't lie, and doesn't care about a bonus.

I don't know what the form looks like yet.

The forms we have are about humans wanting things. Recognition. Growth. A different desk. The new employee wants none of it.

What you measure is what the manager has to be — and that's the part nobody's quite ready for.
