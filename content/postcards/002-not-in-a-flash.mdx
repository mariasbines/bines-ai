I keep waiting for the AI failure to look like the movie.

There's no red light flashing. Nothing tips over. The model doesn't go rogue and start writing manifestos.

It keeps doing exactly what it was doing yesterday — only quieter, slightly off, to slightly the wrong customer, for slightly the wrong reason.

Not in a flash. In silence.

That's the failure mode no one is plotting for.
