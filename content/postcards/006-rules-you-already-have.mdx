The senior partner has the risk appetite. The Slack channel has the process nuance. The PDF has the policy.

None of them are written in a way an agent can read.

Most enterprises don't need new rules. They need to find the old ones and translate them.

The translation is the hard part. The rules were never the bottleneck.
