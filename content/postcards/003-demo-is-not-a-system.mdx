A demo is not a system.

A system is the demo plus everything you didn't show. Compliance. Logging. The thing that has to keep working at 3am when the on-call engineer is offline.

Vibe coding fixes the demo and ships nothing else. Then the room claps. Then the same room is angry six months later.

I keep thinking about what the room is actually clapping for.
