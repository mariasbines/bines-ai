I keep meeting CTOs who can tell me what their cloud bill was last month and not what it's *for*.

What it cost. Not what it bought.

There's a thing where the question of value gets answered with a number that has no opinion about it. *"$80k."* Of what? Don't worry about it.

Cost isn't the enemy. Cost is the mirror.

I just don't think we've all looked.
