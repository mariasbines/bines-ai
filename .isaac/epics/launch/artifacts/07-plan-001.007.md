## Design decisions

1. **Frontmatter values** (AC-002): `number: 1`, `published: "2026-04-22"`, `tags: ["meta", "ai"]`. No explicit accent — falls back to `1 % 5 = 1` → sapphire (checks out; postcard #001 being sapphire-accented is fine).
2. **Signoff handling**: the state-of-play draft ends with `*maria · 22 apr*`. In the rendered postcard we use `<PostcardCard>`/`<PostcardArticle>` which auto-append `maria · dd mmm` from the `published` date. To avoid double-signoff, we DROP the signoff line from the MDX body and let the renderer handle it.

   **Alternative considered and rejected:** keep the signoff in body + override renderer. Cleaner to let the renderer do it uniformly for all postcards.

3. **Body content**: the three paragraphs minus the signoff. "POSTCARD #001" header is auto-rendered by the component, so drop that too (it's metadata, not body).
