### Medium (3)

**M-001 · Vercel Blob append pattern races under concurrent submissions**

- Severity: MEDIUM
- File: Plan Step 2
- Details: The get-then-concat-then-put pattern is not atomic. Two simultaneous requests can both read the same baseline + both write their own concatenation, losing one entry. Plan acknowledges this is v1-acceptable.
- Impact: Low for v1 traffic. Worth noting.
- Suggested fix: Accept for v1; post-launch move to per-submission files (`push-back/YYYY-MM-DD/{uuid}.json`) if real concurrency materialises.

**M-002 · `edge` runtime + `@vercel/blob` — verify token + fetch work under edge**

- Severity: MEDIUM
- File: Plan Step 2 (runtime) + Step 4 (route)
- Details: `@vercel/blob`'s `put`/`list` use HTTP fetch under the hood, which works on edge. The `BLOB_READ_WRITE_TOKEN` env var is readable on edge. OK.
- Suggested fix: None.

**M-003 · `FieldworkArticle` "push back on this piece" footer — out of story scope?**

- Severity: MEDIUM
- File: Plan Step 6
- Details: Story's "Files to Modify" includes `FieldworkArticle.tsx`. Adding a footer push-back control is in-scope. Verified.
- Suggested fix: None.
