# Concept: bines.ai — launch

## Summary

Personal site for Maria Bines (CEO of SynapseDx). Deliberately distinct from her SynapseDx CEO brand — a surface for her own voice, opinions, and aesthetic on AI × life, as a practitioner not a pundit. Long-form "Fieldwork" pieces in rotation, short-form numbered "Postcards," a first-class AI chat interface ("argue with me"), and a visible, honestly-organised archive.

**`~/Documents/bines-ai-brainstorm/02-state-of-play.md`** — full concept doc capturing voice, aesthetic, content architecture, front-door copy, first Fieldwork piece draft, and Postcard #001 draft. That file is the canonical concept; this file references it rather than duplicates it.

## Purpose

Position Maria as a thought leader on AI × life without the SynapseDx CEO framing. Target brand posture: *"want to be in her orbit."* Fun · smart · a little provocative · with depth. British restraint + Southern storytelling DNA + Canadian-ish wry cadence (Maria is Kentucky-born, London-based since ~2016, now sounds Canadian).

Also an explicit forcing function to get Maria comfortable with video.

## Surfaces (content architecture)

- **Fieldwork** — long-form argument-style pieces, 4-6 in rotation at any time. Each rendered as a card with metadata strip: *published · media · pushback · change-my-mind · tags*. CTAs: `[ watch ] [ read ] [ push back ]`.
- **Postcards** — short numbered observations (Chuck Lorre vanity-card lineage). Higher frequency than Fieldwork.
- **/now** — monthly. Currently obsessed with / failing at / rereading.
- **Taste shelf** — 3-5 current reads/watches/listens.
- **Antipatterns strip** — the *"I don't do"* list.
- **Archive** — organised by status (not chronology): *still right / evolved / changed my mind*. "Changed my mind" is rare and first-class; one per year expected.
- **"Argue with me"** — first-class AI chat interface that lets visitors argue with an AI persona in Maria's voice.

## Voice

Diagnostic, not confessional. Observations, not feelings-prose. Contrarian take first, leave a question hanging. Do not improvise voice — consult the state-of-play doc and existing Fieldwork pieces.

## Aesthetic

Editorial-maximalist. Saul Bass / Sister Corita Kent / Matisse cutouts / Charley Harper lineage. Saturated limited palette, big shape-and-colour blocks, scale not volume. NOT SynapseDx palette. NO default AI-image tropes.

## Media

- AI-generated atmospheric video loops for headers (2-3s, silent autoplay, captioned in deliberate style)
- Low-fi Maria-to-camera for argument testimonials (cringe-worthy OK, intentional)
- Principle: *the machine makes the scenery, the human makes the points.*
