**M-002 · `priority` video without `fetchpriority="high"` hint**

- Severity: MEDIUM
- File: Step 1 `<VideoLoop>` priority path
- Details: Plan sets `preload='auto'` for priority videos but not `fetchpriority='high'`. For true LCP hero video, `fetchpriority="high"` on the video element (+ on the poster `<img>` preload) helps browser prioritisation. Next 15 supports this on video attrs.
